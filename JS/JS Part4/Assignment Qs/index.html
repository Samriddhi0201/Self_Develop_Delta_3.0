Q4. Print the Factorial of a number n.
Factorial of an number is the product of all positive integers less than or equal to a given positive integer and denoted by that integer.]
Example:
7! (factorial of 7) = 1x2x3x4x5x6x7 = 5040
5! (factorial of 5) = 1x2x3x4x5 = 120
3! ( factorial of 3) = 1x2x3= 6
0! Is always 1
